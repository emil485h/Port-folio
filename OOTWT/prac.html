Practical Information

Out Of The World Travels will take you around the moon in approximately 7 days.

On the day of departure please arrive 3 hour before lift of.

In some of our more advanced packages you will also get a week of intensive and fun astronaut training! This is to prepare you for the trip and give you an idea of life in space. It will amongst other things consist of:

  • A trip in a Northrop T-38 Talon Jet
  • Fitness training like an astronaut
  • Tour of the controlcenter
  • Spacewarking with the ARGOS sytem

When in space we recommend you use 2 hours a day on the training bike in the aircraft in order to keep fit and avoid future health issues in connection with space travel.

We are not responsible for any loss of property or health damages such as but not limited to osteoporosis, backpain, visual impairment, weakened immune system or psychological traumas.
